About Us

Home › About Us

Ridham Medicare is engaged in manufacturing and supplying different types of jigs. The list of jigs that we provide comprises tibia jig, PFN jig, ankle nail jig, distal femur jig, drill sleeve, femur jig, impactor bolt, moveable spanner, PFN conical bolt, recon jig, etc. The jigs that we provide are manufactured from high purity alumina with very high precision. We have a team of dedicated & skilled engineers and workers who strive hard to produce flawless jigs. Our jigs are widely known for their accurate dimensions, high strength and corrosion resistance. These jigs are mainly used for heat treatment of material in the furnace. The products that we are offering are unmatched in terms of quality and durability. Our jigs are available in different sizes and specifications as well. Customers can place their orders and rest assured for timely delivery and reasonable prices.

Ridham Medicare is a Rajkot (Gujarat, India) based company, which was incorporated in the year 2004. Under the guidance of experienced mentors, we have emerged as a notable name in the field of orthopedic implants and instruments.

We have set up a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility that enables us to manufacture best-in-class jigs. It is equipped with modern technology and advanced tools & equipment, which help us in enhancing our production capacity.
